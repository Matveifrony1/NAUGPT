CALL FOR PAPERS

The Review Committee awaits all theoretical, methodological and applied reports presenting contemporary development and problems solving ideas in the field of Ecosystem Restoration.

The Conference Event Plan includes keynote lectures, round table discussions and publication of abstracts and full size papers.

Who can participate: scientists, researches, representatives of authorities, NGOs, local communities and any interested parties ready to share their experience of restoring ecosystems, damaged by any forms of human activity.

We have kept the registration fee at affordable rates to encourage the participation of young upcoming practitioners and scientists.
