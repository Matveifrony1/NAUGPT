Іноземним студентам, аспірантам та слухачам підготовчого відділення НАУ 
Департамент забезпечення якості освіти пропонує 
пройти опитування щодо якості онлайн-навчання.
Dear students!
National Aviation University has switched its educational process to distance learning with the help of Google services. We need your opinion to make the process more effective, to provide development of the existing platform and improvement of the educational level.
Each student has the opportunity to evaluate the quality of the educational process on the basis of Google Classroom platform. To do this, please fill out the
form
.
The questionnaire, developed by the NAU Department of Education Quality Assurance, is anonymous and can be submitted by April 16, 2020.
