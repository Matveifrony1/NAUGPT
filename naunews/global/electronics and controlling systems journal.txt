AIMS AND SCOPE

International scientific journal “Electronics and Control Systems” is intended for publishing and further free distribution (Open Access Policy) of original English language articles, which are scientifically sound and relevant to an international academic and professional audience in the following research fields:

Computer Sciences and Information Technologies
Computer Engineering
Power Machinery
Automation and Computer-integrated Technologies
Electronics
Telecommunications and Radio Engineering
Aviation Transport
Articles, which are outside the Aims and Scope, are not accepted by the journal for publishing.

The articles are published only in English since 2013.

PUBLISHING SHEDULE

The journal publishes 4 issues per year in accordance with the publishing schedule as follows.

The first issue of the year is published in April of the current year.

The second issue of the year is published in July of the current year.

The third issue of the year is published in October of the current year.

The fourth issue of the year is published in January of the next year.
